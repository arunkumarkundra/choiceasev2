Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information we do not collect

Choicease is a static web application. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information — no names, no email addresses, no browsing history, no analytics.

2. Your data

Everything you create stays on your device. Analysis runs entirely in your browser. Optional draft autosave uses your browser's local storage only, and exports (PDF, CSV, JSON, QR) are generated locally.

3. Cookies and tracking

We use no cookies, no analytics tools, and no third-party trackers.

4. Third-party services

Choicease is hosted on GitHub Pages, and fonts and libraries are served from CDNs. Those providers may log minimal technical information (such as IP addresses) for security and operations, per their own privacy statements.

Questions? contact@choicease.com
