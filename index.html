How Choicease works

Choicease turns a hard call into a defensible recommendation in six short steps — the same weighted multi-criteria method consultants use, without the spreadsheet.

  1. Frame the decision in one sentence.
  2. List options — the candidates on the table.
  3. Set criteria — what actually decides it.
  4. Weigh each criterion's importance; weights normalize to 100% automatically.
  5. Rate every option on every criterion (0–5).
  6. Verdict — a recommendation with confidence, drivers, flip points, and live what-if sliders.

Then export a board-ready PDF, the underlying CSV, or a QR/JSON snapshot you can re-import and rework any time.

  • Private by design. Everything runs in your browser; nothing is uploaded.
  • Honest by design. Confidence is graded, assumptions are visible, and sensitivity analysis shows exactly when the answer would flip.
