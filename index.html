🧭 How Choicease works

Stuck in decision limbo? We'll walk you out, one easy step at a time — and hand you an answer you can actually trust.

  1. Frame it: tell us the decision (or import a saved one).
  2. List options: everything on the table.
  3. Set criteria: what matters. 💡 Our free AI helper can spark ideas.
  4. Weigh them: rate importance 1–5; we normalize to 100%.
  5. Rate options: score each option on each criterion.
  6. Results: a clear winner, honest warnings when it's close, and a whole advanced-analytics world when you want to geek out. 📊
