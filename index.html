🧭 How Choicease works

Stuck in decision limbo? We'll walk you out, one easy step at a time, to arrive at an answer you can actually trust.

  1. Frame it: tell us the decision.
  2. List options: everything on the table.
  3. Set criteria: what matters to you. 💡 Free AI help if you're stuck.
  4. Weigh your criteria: rate how much each one matters, from 1 to 5. 💡 AI help here too.
  5. Rate options: score each option on each criterion. 💡 Yes, AI can pitch in here as well.
  6. Results: a clear winner — and a whole advanced-analytics playground when you want to geek out. 📊
